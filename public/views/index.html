Our Story

Alvin, Mike, Prasangi, and Shaz wanted to know if there was a location they could sell treasured items securely. Surely, there was someone who would purchase their designer goods. Since there wasn't a website or outlet for this service , they decided to create their own. They worked tirelessly at coffee shops and slept on the couch to develop their site. On May 7th, 2018, with a team of just four people, Posh officially launched. Today, Posh serves thousands of the coolest customers, and has secured backing from iconic investors like Bill Gates and Tim Cook. Posh has been covered by Good Morning America, The Today Show, The New York Times, TIME, and more. We’re well on our way to changing the way people buy and sell designer handbags, but really, we’re just getting started. Our model is unique due to its carefully curated catalogue of desirable items, that once submitted for sale, are expertly checked for 100% quality and authenticity. Every day new items are submitted by our community of sellers, which enables buyers to hunt amongst over 3,200 coveted and must have fashion pieces a day. As an up and coming company, our values are our driving force for success because we think collective: we work with style, with entrepreneurial spirit and with passion.
